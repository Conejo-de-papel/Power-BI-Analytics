# EPAM Systems Acquisition Case Study

## Overview
This project analyzes EPAM Systems as a potential acquisition target for Accenture. The goal is to expand Accenture’s market share in the consulting and technology space in the USA.

## Key Analysis Areas
- **Company Overview**: Background, market position, recent developments.
- **Services & Workforce**: Key offerings, industry focus, employee distribution.
- **Financials & Strategic Fit**: Revenue trends, profitability.

## Deliverables
- A 3-slide deck summarizing key insights.

## Timeline
- **Total duration**: 3 hours
  - **Research & analysis**: 2 hours
  - **Slide preparation & review**: 1 hour
